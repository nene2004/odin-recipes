Ravioli

Ravioli, a classic Italian dish, is a type of pasta filled with a variety of delicious ingredients such as ricotta cheese, spinach, or ground meat.

Each ravioli is like a little pocket of flavor, carefully sealed in a delicate pasta dough. Traditionally served with a rich tomato sauce or a velvety butter and sage sauce, ravioli offers a perfect balance between the tender pasta and the savory filling inside. Whether you're making it from scratch or enjoying a pre-made version, ravioli is a comforting and versatile dish that can be tailored to suit any taste

Ingredients

  • pre-made ravioli
  • Chicken thigh
  • Bacon
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Tomato
  • White wine
  • Heavy cream
  • Spices
  • Ginger & Garlic

Steps

  1. Fry or oven your bacon
  2. Spice you chicken thigh or any choice of protein till desired
  3. Fry spiced chicken thigh
  4. Boil your pre made ravioli for 5 minutes and set aside some pasta water
  5. In the same pan the chicken thigh was fried on, fry onions, pepper and tomatoes
  6. In the frying onions add ginger and garlic then the pepper and then the tomatoes with some paprika
  7. Add some Maggi and other spices (pepper maybe chilli)
  8. Add some white wine and let boil for a while
  9. Taste and after boiling , add heavy cream and let boil
  10. Taste and add more spice if needed
  11. Put in your boiled ravioli,little pasta water and stir
  12. Add your protein and stir
  13. Add bacon if added and viola
